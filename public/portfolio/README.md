## Image Guidelines

- **Format**: JPG or PNG
- **Size**: Recommended 1000x1000px to 2000x2000px
- **Quality**: High resolution for best display
- **Content**: Your best tattoo work showcasing different styles and placements

The bento grid will automatically arrange them in varying sizes for visual interest.
